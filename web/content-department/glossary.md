# Philosophical Glossary — 朝阳体操队

> *Key terms, concepts, and their translations for the documentary book.*

## Core Philosophical Terms

| # | Chinese | Japanese (Rōmaji) | English Translation | Context |
|---|---------|-------------------|---------------------|---------|
| 1 | 异体同心 | *Itai Dōshin* | Different Bodies, One Heart/Mind | The foundational principle — many individuals function as one organism |
| 2 | 师弟不二 | *Shitei Funi* | Master and Disciple, Inseparable | The eternal, non-dual bond between teacher and student |
| 3 | 广宣流布 | *Kōsen Rufu* | World peace through individual happiness | The mission to spread Buddhist humanism globally |
| 4 | 御本尊 | *Gohonzon* | Object of devotion | The Nichiren Buddhist mandala, object of faith |
| 5 | 题目 | *Daimoku* | Chanting Nam-myoho-renge-kyo | The core practice — vocal invocation of the Lotus Sutra's title |
| 6 | 勤行 | *Gongyō* | Morning/evening prayer | The twice-daily recitation practice |
| 7 | 人间革命 | *Ningen Kakumei* | Human Revolution | Inner transformation of the individual |
| 8 | 地涌菩萨 | *Jiyu no Bosatsu* | Bodhisattvas of the Earth | The disciples who emerge to propagate the teaching |
| 9 | 虚空会 | *Kokū-e* | Ceremony in the Air | The assembly described in the Lotus Sutra |
| 10 | 师之魂 | *Shi no Tamashii* | Soul of the Teacher | The disciple embodying the mentor's spirit |
| 11 | 外护 | *Gaigo* | External guardianship | Cultural groups protecting the organization externally |
| 12 | 内护 | *Naigo* | Internal guardianship | Security groups protecting internally |
| 13 | 牙城 | *Gajō* | Citadel / Fortress | The organization as a protected spiritual stronghold |
