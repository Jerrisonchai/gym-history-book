# Act V: Future Vision — Senior Guidance

> *"Continue training with faith as your foundation. Through this, you will become outstanding talents in every field."*

## 5.1 Words for the Next Generation

The following guidance comes from senior leaders who built the foundation. These are not theories — they are lived truths.

### On Spirit

> *"The Gymnastics Troupe must become the embodiment of the organization's spirit. Every Men's Division member must be capable of enduring hardship. From a parent's perspective, a gymnastics troupe performance must be a performance that puts hearts at ease."*

### On Faith

> *"The Gymnastics Troupe is a faith-based organization. It represents the spirit of the organization, the spirit of Soka, the spirit of the Men's Division. And that spirit is the spirit of the Master-Disciple Bond — 师弟不二."*

### On Purpose

> *"The Gymnastics Troupe was established for peace, for society, gathering good-hearted and capable people together. May every member learn what constitutes a correct outlook on life, correct values — and through this, awaken to their own mission."*

### On the Tower

> *"The gymnastics troupe's tower is the Treasure Tower of Life — the Treasure Tower that appears in the Ceremony in the Air. This symbolizes that every one of us is a participant in that ceremony, a Bodhisattva of the Earth. Awaken to your mission. Through your mission and cultural performances, protect the organization's citadel through external guardianship."*

*(Internal guardians = the organization's security group; external guardians = the cultural groups)*

## 5.2 The Promise

To every future member who steps onto the training ground:

- You are not just learning to climb. You are learning to lead.
- You are not just building towers. You are building character.
- You are not just performing for an audience. You are performing for peace.

Continue in the discipline of the Gymnastics Troupe. With faith as the foundation, you will surely become an outstanding talent in your field, making the greatest contribution to the organization and the nation.
