# Act III: Modern Era (2000s–Present)

> *"A name is a destiny."*

## 3.1 The Naming — 2004

In 2004, the highest honor was bestowed: **Ikeda Sensei personally named the SGM Gymnastics Troupe the "朝阳体操队"** (Chaoyang Gymnastics Team — literally "Morning Sun Gymnastics Team"). 

The name 朝阳 (Morning Sun) carries deep meaning:
- **朝** — the dawn, the rising sun
- **阳** — positive, yang energy, illumination

The name signified that this team was now recognized not merely as performers, but as bearers of a spiritual tradition — bringing light and hope through their art.

## 3.2 The Video Era

With the advancement of technology, documentation shifted from VHS tapes to digital video. Performances could now be shared instantly, studied frame-by-frame, and archived permanently. This expanded the team's reach and raised performance standards across all state chapters.

## 3.3 Continuing the Mission

The modern era saw continued performances at:
- National and international events
- Government invitations and ceremonies
- SGM cultural festivals of increasing scale
- Community outreach programs

## 3.4 What Changed — and What Didn't

| Changed | Unchanged |
|---------|-----------|
| Technology (digital video) | The 4-level tower as the core |
| Scale of documentation | Master-disciple relationship |
| Broader government recognition | 5 AM *gongyo* before training |
| International visibility | *Different Bodies, One Heart* |

The tower remains. The spirit remains. The morning sun rises again.
