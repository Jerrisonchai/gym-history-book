## 📋 DATA REQUEST — Alumni Quotes Template

The most powerful material for this book will be **first-person quotes** from members. If you or alumni can provide quotes for these prompts, they'll go directly into the Acts:

### For Act I Veterans (1976–1990)
- "What do you remember most about the first Cultural Festival?"
- "Describe the moment the 5-level tower stood up for the first time."
- "What was it like performing in the rain in Johor 1989?"
- "What do you remember about SEA Games 1989 — the 5 AM gongyo, the 19 towers?"

### For Act II Veterans (1990s–2000s)
- "How did Softgym change the way you trained?"
- "What was the first national competition like?"
- "Describe a Sunday visit — traveling by bus to encourage fellow members."
- "What was your favorite formation or routine?"

### For All Generations
- "What does '异体同心' mean to you personally?"
- "What did you gain from the gymnastics troupe that you still carry today?"
- "Describe a moment when you almost gave up — and what made you continue."
- "What do you want future generations to know about 朝阳?"
