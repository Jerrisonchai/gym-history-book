# GYMBOY 核心价值体系 (Core Values Framework)

> **来源/Source:** Jerrison Chai, 2026-06-26
> **用途/Purpose:** Act IV — Spirit & Philosophy, 朝阳体操队 Documentary Book
> **结构/Structure:** 3 Levels — Basic → Intermediate → Advanced

---

## Level 1: Basic (基础)

| # | Principle (中文) | English Translation |
|---|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1.1 | **心为确要** — 一切以心为重要，用心、认真去练习 | The heart/mind is paramount — practice with sincerity and focus |
| 1.2 | **水的信心** — 不能三分钟热度，必须像水流般，不停努力到成功为止 | Faith like water — persistent effort, flowing continuously until success |
| 1.3 | **声为佛事** — 声音的力量从事佛的工作，敢敢地喊出来 | Voice as Buddhist practice — shout out counts and commands with conviction |
| 1.4 | **佛法讲胜负** — 挑战并赢得胜利就是GYM练习、表演的目的 | Buddhism is about victory — winning through challenge is the purpose of training |
| 1.5 | **心之财第一也！** — 一生都要追求心之财，磨练信心，磨练人格 | Treasures of the heart are foremost — pursue inner wealth, forge character |
| 1.6 | **己心之魔、第六天魔王** — 看透魔，与魔战斗到底 | See through inner devils and fight them to the end |
| 1.7 | **男子部口号: "率先行动，赢取胜利"** | Men's Division motto: "Take initiative, win victory" |
| 1.8 | **破邪显正** — 为正义而奋斗，永远站在正义一方 | Refute the erroneous, reveal the true — always stand on the side of justice |
| 1.9 | **鸟有鸟的道路，鱼有鱼的道路，人有人的道路** — GYM有使命，必须走完全程 | Birds, fish, humans each have their path — GYM has its mission, must complete it |
| 1.10 | **菩萨=能忍** — 每个男子部一定要能吃苦，这就是菩萨的修行 | Bodhisattva = endurance — every man must bear hardship as Bodhisattva practice |
| 1.11 | **马来西亚 · 世界第一** — Sensei对每个人的期待 | Malaysia · World #1 — Sensei's expectation for everyone |
| 1.12 | **一人立起** — 黑暗愈深，黎明愈近 | One person stands — the darker the night, the closer the dawn |
| 1.13 | **樱梅桃李不改个个当体** — 自己终究是自己 | Cherry, plum, peach, damson — each blooms as itself, never as another |
| 1.14 | **大恶发生则大善一定会来** — 不到最后一分钟，绝不能放弃 | Great evil heralds great good — never give up until the very last moment |
| 1.15 | **报恩抄: "老狐不忘故丘"** — 忘恩为畜生，感恩为人 | Gratitude — never forget your roots; ingratitude is beastly |
| 1.16 | **由镰仓去京都十二日路程…行十一日而停步，怎能看京都的月亮？** | The 12-day journey — stop at day 11 and you'll never see Kyoto's moon |
| 1.17 | **苦悟其苦、乐开其乐，苦乐与共，同唱南无妙法莲华经可也** | Awaken to suffering, open to joy — share both, chant together |

## Level 2: Intermediate (中级)

| # | Principle (中文) | English Translation |
|---|-----------------|---------------------|
| 2.1 | **朝阳 = 师弟** — 太阳就如师匠，弟子朝着光的方向不怕晒、拼命学习 | Morning Sun = Master & Disciple — the sun is the mentor, we face the light without fear |
| 2.2 | **师弟不二** — 师匠与弟子成为一体，以师心为己心 | Master and disciple, inseparable — breathe as one, move as one |
| 2.3 | **Tower 的意义** — 生命的宝塔，虚空会的宝塔，和Sensei誓愿建起胜利的宝塔 | The Tower's meaning — the treasure tower of life, vowed with Sensei |
| 2.4 | **石虎将军** — 射箭穿石的「强盛信心」 | Stone Tiger General — the power of unwavering faith |
| 2.5 | **狮子王之心 = 太阳之心** — 一人奋起的勇气之心 | Lion King's heart = Sun's heart — the courage to rise alone |
| 2.6 | **湿木取火, 干土出水** — 强盛一念，全力祈求 | Fire from wet wood, water from dry earth — absolute determination |
| 2.7 | **诸法实相、色心不二** — 心不在焉在行动中看得到 | Body and mind are not two — absent mind is visible in movement |
| 2.8 | **发迹显本** — 除去凡夫之相，显现原本的佛之生命 | Revealing the true self — shedding the ordinary to reveal the Buddha nature |
| 2.9 | **石烧之成灰，而金烧之成真金** — 如果你石头，磨练出来就只是灰 | Stone burns to ash, gold refines to pure gold — what you are determines what you become |
| 2.10 | **日日月月加强信心，稍有松懈，必受魔侵** | Strengthen faith daily — slacken even slightly, and devils attack |
| 2.11 | **户田禁止核武器宣言** — 拔除潜藏在核武器之中的魔爪 | Toda's anti-nuclear declaration — remove the devil's claws hidden in nuclear weapons |
| 2.12 | **户田青年训: 創造新世紀的是青年的熱與力** | Toda's youth guidance: "Youth's passion and power create the new century" |
| 2.13 | **如瀑布般激昂、不懈、无畏、明朗、威武；男子汉要有王者风范** | Like a waterfall — fierce, tireless, fearless, bright, commanding; men must have regal bearing |
| 2.14 | **成水鱼之思、异体同心** — 团结的力量 | Like fish in water, different bodies one heart — the power of unity |
| 2.15 | **在在诸佛土，常与师具生** — 不管什么时代，永远和师匠没有距离 | In every Buddha land, always born with the teacher — never apart across time |

## Level 3: Advanced (高级)

| # | Principle (中文) | English Translation |
|---|-----------------|---------------------|
| 3.1 | **去浅就深,丈夫之心也** | Abandon the shallow, pursue the profound — the heart of a true man |
| 3.2 | **大将军畏缩,则步兵不前** | When the general flinches, the soldiers advance no further |
| 3.3 | **三变土田** — 释尊净化那由他的国土 | Three transformations of land — the Buddha purifies countless lands |
| 3.4 | **开目抄: 我志已决，不仰诸天，身承诸难，誓终此生** | Opening of the Eyes: "My resolve is set — I seek no heaven's aid, I bear all hardship, to my last breath" |
| 3.5 | **所持之法若是第一，所持之人亦必是第一** | If the Law you uphold is foremost, you who uphold it are foremost |
| 3.6 | **御义口传: 一念尽亿劫之辛劳** — 胜利的人确信自己必定获胜 | A single moment of resolve contains eons of effort — victors know they will win |
| 3.7 | **勇猛精进** (Yumyo shojin) | Courageous and diligent practice |
| 3.8 | **众生所游乐** (Shujo sho yu-raku) | Living beings delight in play |
| 3.9 | **一心欲见佛** (Isshin yok ken butsu) | Single-mindedly desiring to see the Buddha |
| 3.10 | **速成就佛身** (Soku joju bushin) | Quickly attain Buddhahood |
| 3.11 | **四行菩萨的姿态 = 常乐我净** | Four Bodhisattva virtues: Eternity, Joy, True Self, Purity |
| 3.12 | **立正安国论: 苍蝇附骥尾而渡万里** — 污浊生命的我们因GYM踏上师弟之道 | Fly on a thoroughbred's tail crosses ten thousand miles — we, ordinary beings, ride the master-disciple path |
| 3.13 | **老鹰天空之王，法华经经中之王** — 100%确信GYMBOY哲理第一，百战百胜 | Eagle is sky's king, Lotus Sutra is sutras' king — 100% conviction, GYMBOY philosophy is foremost |
| 3.14 | **一眼龜在海中值遇浮木的困難** — 参与GYM的机会渺小如单眼龟遇浮木，要珍惜一切 | The one-eyed turtle and the floating log — treasure this rare chance to be in GYM |
