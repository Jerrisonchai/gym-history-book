# Act IV: Spirit & Philosophy

> *"The tower is not just a tower. It is the Treasure Tower that appears in the sky — and we are all part of it."*
> — Senior guidance

## 4.1 What Is Gymnastics Troupe Spirit?

### Pillar One: The Men's Division Spirit

The Gymnastics Troupe is an expression of the **Soka Gakkai Men's Division spirit** — forged through the Chief Director's three guiding principles and a commitment to human development. Through the discipline of gymnastic training, members build unshakeable faith and become successors of the organization.

A gymnastics troupe performance is a **life-to-life exchange**. With the passion and power of youth, it conveys the organization's spirit. The unique quality of our performances is that audiences feel the *masculine spirit* — yet beyond that, the performance encourages and inspires people.

**Key Source References:** 青年训 (Youth Training), 新人间革命 (New Human Revolution), 常胜 (Ever-Victorious)

### Pillar Two: Different Bodies, One Heart (异体同心)

The spirit of *Different Bodies, One Heart* encompasses:
- Perseverance and resilience
- Collective struggle toward a shared goal
- Relentless pursuit of victory
- Everything grounded in the master-disciple relationship

This is why the gymnastics troupe has historically been the **closing act** of cultural festivals — it embodies the visual and spiritual peak: youth's passion, combined with high-difficulty performance, creating an unforgettable climax.

### Pillar Three: Tower as Life's Treasure Tower

The tower is a direct symbol of the relationship with Ikeda Sensei. When we build the tower, we are building the **Tower of Victory** — enacting the vow we share with our mentor.

The *New Human Revolution* recounts the rain-soaked cultural festival in Kansai, where a six-level tower rose like the Treasure Tower of Life, with the words *"Shi no Tamashii"* (Soul of the Teacher) as backdrop — signifying that the disciple must become the mentor's soul, fighting alongside him forever. There is no distance between us and our mentor.

## 4.2 What Members Gain

**First:** In the gymnastics troupe, we learn to eternally accept change. With powerful resolve, we determine life's victory. Never relax until the very last moment.

**Second:** We learn the spirit of never giving up. What matters most is achieving the final victory.

**Third:** As leaders, we learn organizational management — how to handle members with different personalities, treating each person uniquely. Every challenge is approached as a new learning process, facing greater future challenges with courage.
